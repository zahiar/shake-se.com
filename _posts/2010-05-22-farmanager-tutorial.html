Now lets get to work

Extract the downloaded zip file and run the file "Far.exe"

Once the program has loaded, press "F11" on the keyboard, to load the pop-up menu, which shows you all the plug-ins

Click on "SEFP2", this is the plugin which lets you access your phone

Click on "Connect", to start the connection process

Turn off your phone, then press and hold the "C" button on your phone's keypad. Whilst holding the button, plugin the cable.
If the "C" button does not work, you can try holding the buttons "2" & "5" together.

Navigate to where your menu is stored on your computer using the left hand column.

Navigate to /tpa/preset/system/desktop/flash using the right hand column.

NB ".." - if you click this, then you will go to the parent folder.

Click the menu you want to upload on the left hand column, and drag it onto the right hand column

Click on "Copy", which will upload a copy of the file onto your phone

Now click on "Quit", when you have finished uploading all the menus onto your phone

Click on "Yes" to exit the program

That concludes the tutorial, if you want to link this flash menu to a different theme, then view this tutorial and skip to part 2
