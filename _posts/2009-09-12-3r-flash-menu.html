Here's a new menu. This has password protection and can now rotate three different ways.

The menu will also change colour throughout the day, it will go from red in the morning, to blue in the afternoon, and finally to green at night.

As before the menu will work on all phones, if the menu doesn't work, then let me know. I have uploaded two different versions of the menu below, one which has the password protection feature, and the other one doesn't.

Update (09-14-2009):

Thanks to Tom for reporting a bug in the menu, where the menu switches icons when you press 4/6. I have fixed the bug and have re-created all the menus including the customised ones.

Update (09-17-2009):
